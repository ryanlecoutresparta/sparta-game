Squares
HOW TO PLAY
You control the blue square, which you move with the up, down, left and right arrow keys.
Avoid the red squares - if you touch one, you lose one of your lives and get sent back to the start of the level!
Collect all of the coins in each level to progress to the next one!
When you see a life square, grab it to gain an extra life!
You have 30 seconds per level - collect as many coins as you can. Good luck!
